Proceedings of Data Science Africa Frequently Asked Questions

The Proceedings of Data Science Africa are very much a community effort.

How do I publish a paper with you?

Paper publications are handled through pull requests on Github. There are three main stages.

  • Fork the repo containing the current submission volume.
  • Create a new file under the _posts directory named YYYY-MM-DD-paper-title.md where YYYY is the current year (Gregorian calendar), MM is the current month in digits, DD is the day of the current month, starting with 0 if it is one of the first 10 days. paper-title is the title of your paper in lower case, with - replacing punctuation and spaces, e.g. "Malaria Distribution in Uganda" becomes "malaria-distribution-in-uganda".
  • Write your paper using *markdown*, see paper formatting instructions here.
  • When your paper is ready for submission, submit a *pull request*. You will be assigned a mentor who will work through your paper with you using Github's code review tools. When the paper is ready, the pull request will be merged.
